# My Profile - Business Document

## Product Name
**Emmanuel Hagan — Personal Portfolio Website**

---

## Executive Summary

A personal portfolio website showcasing Emmanuel Hagan's skills, projects, and experience as a Full Stack Developer. This is not a revenue-generating product — it is a professional branding and lead generation tool that drives freelance opportunities, job offers, and business partnerships.

---

## Purpose

1. **Professional presence** — Establish credibility as a full-stack developer
2. **Project showcase** — Display completed and ongoing projects to potential clients/employers
3. **Contact funnel** — Single URL to share in applications, LinkedIn, Twitter, business cards
4. **SEO** — Rank for "Emmanuel Hagan developer" and related searches
5. **Proof of work** — Demonstrate technical ability through the portfolio itself

---

## Target Audience

| Audience | What They Look For | Call to Action |
|----------|-------------------|----------------|
| Recruiters / HR | Skills, experience, tech stack | Download resume, contact form |
| Potential clients | Project examples, reliability | Contact for freelance work |
| Investors / partners | Vision, portfolio of products | Reach out for collaboration |
| Developer community | Open source contributions | GitHub link, blog posts |

---

## Current State

- **Tech:** React 17 (Create React App), react-router-dom, react-typical
- **Type:** Static single-page application
- **Hosting:** Not yet deployed (should be live)
- **Content:** Resume/CV content, project showcase

## Recommendations

### Must-Do (Immediate)
1. **Deploy to Vercel or Netlify** — Takes 5 minutes, free, custom domain support
2. **Buy a custom domain** — emmanuelhagan.dev or emmanuelhagan.com (~$10/year)
3. **Add projects section** — Link to live demos of AltarBot, AdomPon, Akwaaba, etc.
4. **Add downloadable resume** — PDF download button (file already exists in project)
5. **SEO meta tags** — Title, description, OG image for social sharing

### Nice-to-Have (Later)
6. **Blog section** — Write about your tech stack choices, African tech, building products
7. **Testimonials** — Quotes from clients or collaborators
8. **Analytics** — Add Plausible or Umami (privacy-friendly, free self-hosted)
9. **Dark mode** — Modern developer portfolios typically support it
10. **Performance** — Consider migrating from CRA to Vite or Next.js for better performance
